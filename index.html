TechTV Video Recovery

November 16,2015

MIT Office of Digital Learning

On November 3,2015,TechTV suffered a loss of all video data.  The Office of Digital Learning has backups of a subset of the content.  Requests for recovery of content should be made <LINK HERE>.

Video recovery destination policy:

All recovered Tech TV content will be restored to YouTube,unless the end-user has a specific need which requires otherwise.  For users who need controlled access,or video access where YouTube is blocked,ODL can upload video to the provisionally operational TechTV system.  Exigent needs may be accommodated by approval of ODL management.

The use of YouTube as the primary recovery destination will allow digitized formats to be in the highest quality possible,while also easing transition from TechTV.

Video recovery priority policy:

Post-emergency recovery,ODL will prioritize video recovery for requests in this order:

1. Ongoing MIT courses and academic end-users
2. Urgent administrative end-users (president's office,departments,labs,and centers)
3. Publicity and outreach (news office,liaison offices)
4. Other end-users

Video upload policy:

ODL recommends that end-users upload videos to YouTube,or,if MIT-specific access restrictions are needed,to the MIT Google Apps service (using Google Drive),or to the MIT Dropbox service,both of which allow group-specific access control,and sharing of uploaded videos.
